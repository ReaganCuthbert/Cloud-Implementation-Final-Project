About EDMCentral

Reagan, Founder

Hey guys! I want to welcome you to EDMCentral. This blog is to talk about some of the festivals, events, and locations for the EDM community. Feel free to message us on the contact page about anything. - Reagan

EDMCentral was created on April 19th, 2020 to be a place for Electronic Dance Music Fans to be able to discover and get more information about all sorts of events happening for the EDM community.
